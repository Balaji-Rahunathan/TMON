Creating patient card for TMON

  1. In patients timeline status, ESRD Treatment changed to “HD” as seen below

  2. HD prescription updated with Machine, treatment parameters, consumables, fluid , anticoagulant and vascular access ( refer to the document EMED_Settings_For_TMON)

Importing data with password

  1. Once you complete the above steps, open TMON and import basic data as seen below. Enter password when prompted.

Edit patient card data

  1. Next, Choose “Card” menu and Click “Edit Patient Card” , Enter password to confirm.

Previous Next

Select view all patients

  1. Edit patient card pop-up window will open. Check “view all patients “box then select your patient from the list.

Previous Next

Patient card into card reader

  1. Insert a patient card in to the card reader/writer. This is connected to 1 Pc in each unit.

    Please note: If you are re-using a card, name of the patient that is being replaced will be shown in the card information field first. Pleas ensure the correct card type 5008 Patient Card is displayed.

Previous Next

Write selected patient to card

  1. After confirming the selected patient’s details, click “Write Selected patient to card”


  2. Once the write is complete, new patient’s name will appear in the card information. Verify the details, remove the patient and close the Edit patient card window.


  3. Please contact Renal IT on 7320 for any further training or troubleshooting.
